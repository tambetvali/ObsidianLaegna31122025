For example, in the context of Laegna, the relationships between **Person**, **Self**, and **Mind** can be defined in terms of **logical relations** and **functions**, where each element interacts with others through rules that ensure consistency across **dimensions**.

Through these processes, the **machine-provable** system avoids the vagueness of conscious experience and focuses purely on **objective relationships**—such as **inputs, outputs, and mappings**—that can be validated and modeled.
